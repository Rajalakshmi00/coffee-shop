Our History

Founded in a repurposed 1900s warehouse, our coffee shop infuses history with every sip. Amelia's vision transformed this space into a bustling haven. Aromatic brews, cozy ambiance, and community events make it a cherished gathering spot, a testament to coffee's power to connect and create memories

Decades later, in the early 2000s, a passionate entrepreneur named Amelia stumbled upon this forgotten gem. Inspired by her love for coffee and a dream to create a community space, she envisioned transforming this neglected warehouse into a vibrant coffee haven.

Today, as patrons gather to savor their favorite brews amidst the echoes of laughter and conversations, the coffee shop stands as a testament to Amelia's unwavering belief in the power of a simple cup of coffee to bring people together and create lasting memories.
